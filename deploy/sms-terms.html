SolvWorx - SMS

Introduction

SolvWorx operates a system that airports use to manage their PPR reservations. When a pilot calls their FBO to make a reservation, the FBO will ask if the pilot wants SMS or email notifications. If the pilot chooses SMS notifications, they will receive:

  • an initial registration message
  • a message for each reservation or change
  • suspend and resume messages if the airport has a ground-stop
    (including automatically rescheduled departure reservations if necessary)
  • other informational messages at the discretion of the airport manager

The intent of our SMS messaging is entirely transactional as opposed to promotional.
